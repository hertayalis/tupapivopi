Chicago Ridge Mall IL Hours: Everything You Need to Know

If you’re planning a shopping trip to the Chicago Ridge Mall in Illinois, it’s important to know the store/company hours. This ensures that you don’t make a wasted trip or end up waiting outside a closed store. In this article, we’ll cover all the store/company hours for the Chicago Ridge Mall, including holiday hours.

What Time Does the Chicago Ridge Mall Open and Close?

The Chicago Ridge Mall opens at 10:00 am every day and closes at 9:00 pm from Monday to Saturday. On Sunday, the mall opens at 11:00 am and closes at 6:00 pm. However, these hours may vary depending on the store or service, so it’s important to check with the specific store if you have any doubts.

Is Chicago Ridge Mall Open on Saturday and Sunday?

Yes, the Chicago Ridge Mall is open on both Saturday and Sunday. As mentioned previously, although the mall opens at 10:00 am from Monday to Saturday and at 11:00 am on Sunday, the closing times vary.

Chicago Ridge Mall Hours: List of Opening and Closing Times

Here’s a list of Chicago Ridge Mall hours for each day from Monday to Sunday:

  • Monday: 10:00 am – 9:00 pm
  • Tuesday: 10:00 am – 9:00 pm
  • Wednesday: 10:00 am – 9:00 pm
  • Thursday: 10:00 am – 9:00 pm
  • Friday: 10:00 am – 9:00 pm
  • Saturday: 10:00 am – 9:00 pm
  • Sunday: 11:00 am – 6:00 pm

Again, it’s important to note that individual store/company hours may vary, so it’s always best to double-check before making a trip to the mall.

Tips and Tricks for Shopping at the Chicago Ridge Mall

Here are some tips and tricks to help you make the most of your shopping experience:

  • Check the mall directory before you arrive to find the stores you want to visit and their locations in the mall.
  • Plan your trip during less busy hours to avoid crowds and long lines.
  • Consider joining the mall’s VIP Club for exclusive deals and offers.
  • Park in a convenient location, such as near the mall entrance closest to the stores you plan to visit.
  • Wear comfortable shoes and clothing to ensure a more enjoyable shopping experience.
  • If you have children, plan to stop by the mall’s play area or child-friendly stores to keep them entertained.
  • Make sure to take breaks between shopping to hydrate and refuel with snacks or a meal.
  • Lastly, don’t forget to check if the stores you want to visit have their own holiday hours or special offers.
