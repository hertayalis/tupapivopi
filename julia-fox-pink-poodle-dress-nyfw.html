Julia Fox Wore a Cartoon Poodle on Her Sequined Dress for NYFW

 Posted on June 23, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  331 words  |  Martina Birk

There is celebrity street style, and then there's Julia Fox's street style.

This New York Fashion Week, the outspoken actress, model, and self-proclaimed muse has been showing out. Over the week, she has debuted a denim dress with horns by Luis De Javier, a sheer sports jersey, assless chaps with a horse tail, and a sheer white parachute dress and Morph Boots by Elena Velez, to name a few.

Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Fox is a chameleon if we've ever seen one, and her latest look is proof of why we can always rely on her to keep things interesting.

Yesterday, the Uncut Gems star wrapped up Fashion Week in a hot pink midi dress with a furry cartoon poodle right smack on the middle of it. The strapless dress was covered entirely in pink sequins, except for the part taken up by the poodle, where a combination of white faux fur and fuzzy fabric were used—and don't forget the pup's bright blue eyes, long black lashes, and pink lids.

Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

In true style, Fox didn't stop there. She paired the dazzling dress with glossy white sky-high platform heels with crisscrossing straps. She drew a brow line in bright pink over her bleached brows and opted for a slicked-back hairdo and glossy lip.

Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Later in the day, she attended the Willy Chavarria fall 2023 ready-to-wear show at New York's Cooper Hewitt, Smithsonian Design Museum in a see-through black jersey, long basketball shorts, and black leather heeled boots. For the occasion, she debuted fiery red hair, which she matched with her red-lined brows and red-tinted lips.

Christian Vierig//Getty Images
