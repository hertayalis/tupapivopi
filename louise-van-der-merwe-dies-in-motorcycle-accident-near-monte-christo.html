Louise Van Der Merwe, a well-known and respected woman in Mosselbay, South Africa, passed away after a tragic motorcycle accident on February 24, 2024. She was the president of the Velika Charter, a motorcycle club that promotes friendship, adventure, and charity among its members. Louise was also a loving sister, mother, and grandmother to her family and friends.

Meanwhile, Louise was a successful businesswoman who owned and managed several properties in Mosselbay. She was also a passionate motorcycle enthusiast who enjoyed riding and traveling with her club.
