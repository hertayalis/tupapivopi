Madison McKinley- Biography

Madison McKinley is an American model, actress, and television personality who rose to prominence after appearing on the 15th season of “The Bachelor.” She became known as the “fang girl” after arriving on the show wearing fangs, as well as her withdrawing from the competition and allowing fellow participant Emily Maynard to advance after learning her story.

Madison McKinley- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

McKinley was born Madison Garton on March 13, 1986, in Vail, Colorado, where she grew up and attended college. Although nothing is known about her childhood, she wanted to become a model at the age of 16, eventually competing in the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Competition in Manhattan at the age of 18. Although McKinley did not win, she was noticed by several modeling agencies in New York and decided to relocate there to pursue a career in modeling and acting. She also opted to alter her surname to McKinley at the same time.

Madison McKinley- Professional Career

Despite having a steady career as a model and actor, McKinley opted to focus on her personal life in 2011 by joining the hit reality TV show “The Bachelor” for its 15th season. McKinley and 29 other females entered the challenge to win the bachelor Brad Womack’s heart. When she displayed her vampire teeth implants, she acquired the nickname “Fang Girl” — it was discovered that she had a strong love of vampires and even joined the New York Underground Vampire Club when she relocated to the city.

Aside from her passion for vampires, McKinley earned the love and respect of the show’s viewers when she dropped out of the competition in the third week. When she found out what had transpired in fellow participant Emily Maynard’s history, she opted to quit the game freely and enable her to go on. When it was revealed that Maynard’s first love had died in a plane crash, McKinley realized that Maynard required “The Bachelor” more than she did. Madison McKinley’s Film and Television Career

McKinley’s career took off after she appeared on “The Bachelor.” In 2013, she co-starred in the film “The Wolf of Wall Street” with Leonardo DiCaprio and Margot Robbie, and she also appeared as a guest star in the TV series “Orange Is the New Black” the same year. McKinley has also appeared in the television series “30 Rock,” “Rescue Me,” “White Collar,” and “Kings.” She also appeared on the daytime television show “The Ellen DeGeneres Show” and in season four of “Damages.” Her multiple television appearances also contributed to her increasing wealth.

McKinley’s filmography includes appearances in “The Smurfs” 3D film, as well as prominent roles in “Palm Swings” and “Molly’s Game.” She also acted in the Thai film “Realms” and in “Purge of Thrones.” Her work in several films has increased her net worth. McKinley is still working as a model and actress now.
