Logan Paul: Controversy

“The Maverick” Logan has been on and off of several controversies throughout his career, but the biggest of them all came in at the end of 2017.

On December 31, 2017, Logan posted a vlog on his Youtube channel where he was exploring an area inside the Aokigahara Forest of Japan which is infamously named suicide forest.

On reaching there, he and his team found the dead body of a man who recently disappeared in Japan and reportedly hanged himself in the forest.

But instead of turning off the camera and informing the police officials immediately, Logan continued shooting often getting the body inside the same frame as him.

He wanted to shoot his reaction to the incident but was not well received by the public.

He relentlessly shot the footage of the dead man in the forest, which after being uploaded to his channel, received huge public backlash.

Logan later removed the video from his channel, but the damage had been done.

He later apologized for the incident via a video on his channel, with almost 60 million views till now.

The controversy had taken a toll on Logan’s career as many brands, and TV shows had broken their ties with the YouTuber.
