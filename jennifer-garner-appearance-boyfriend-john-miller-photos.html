Jennifer Garner Makes Rare Appearance With Boyfriend John Miller

 Posted on May 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  313 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Jennifer Garner and John Miller are still going strong.

The actor was spotted out jogging her longtime boyfriend in Los Angeles over the weekend.

The two coordinated in black athletic looks, with Garner opting for a black tank and leggings, purple Nike running sneakers, and a light blue baseball hat. She also wore a silver watch. The businessman, meanwhile, wore a black T-shirt from Cali Group (his tech company), gray sweatpants, blue-and-red sneakers, and a black-and-beige baseball hat.

They were seen smiling and chatting as they jogged around Brentwood, where Garner lives.

BACKGRID

Garner and Miller have been dating since 2018 and, despite the actor’s career, have maintained a low-key relationship entirely out of the spotlight. They are rarely seen together in public, and Miller has never walked a Hollywood red carpet with Garner.

Last year, a source told Us Weekly that Garner and Miller keep their relationship “very private,” and that when it comes to date nights, “They meet at each other’s houses—even for a quick glass of wine or for a low-key dinner date night away from prying eyes.”

Garner and Miller don’t live together and will spend the night with one another only “when the timing is right,” the source said, adding that the two have jetted off on “secret getaways” to various places, including New York and Italy, throughout the course of their romance.

Before dating Miller, Garner was married to Ben Affleck from 2005 to 2015. They share kids Violet, 17, Seraphina, 14, and Samuel, 11.
