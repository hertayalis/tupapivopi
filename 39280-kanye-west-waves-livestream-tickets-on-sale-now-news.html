Kanye West &quot;Waves&quot; Live Stream Tickets On Sale Now

 Posted on June 20, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  196 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Kanye West has been continuously teasing the evolving track list for Waves after news came out that the whole album would be streamed along with the reveal for Yeezy Season 3 at a live performance at New York City’s Madison Square Garden. Now tickets are on-sale for the simultaneous live stream of the event, which will be shown at select theaters in over 20 countries around the world.

Vanessa Beecroft, a conceptual artist, is the only other person listed on the even flyer that Kanye West tweeted.

Tickets for the United States and Canada seem to be strangely missing, although there are flags on the event map suggesting tickets will become available. Reddit user thebasedyeezus delved into the source code of the site and discovered that the United States tickets are being hidden because they will become available simultaneously with an album pre-order. No word on when the US pre-order will become available or whether Canada is getting any tickets. Keep it locked here for more info.

Fans are reporting prices to be around $16 (15 euros), which isn't bad considering the cost of everything else Kanye West releases.
