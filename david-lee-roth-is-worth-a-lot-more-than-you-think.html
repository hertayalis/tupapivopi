David Lee Roth Is Worth A Lot More Than You Think

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  212 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Musicians are not always known for their rock-solid financial decisions, especially when it comes to artists whose career peak happened in the 1970s and 1980s, and this is doubly the case with the ever-mercurial David Lee Roth. After all, some of his career decisions don't exactly speak of a man who isn't in sore need of some cash. The Van Halen reunions. The Las Vegas residency. The fact that he at one point worked as an EMT. In fact, Diamond Dave himself has admitted that his net worth has very much been in flux. In 2012, he told the Guardian that he's been on both sides of the poverty line. ""I was a surgical tech right out of high school, I sold clothes," he says. "I shovelled s**t at a horse stable for years. I've been rich and I've been poor. Rich is better." 

You'll notice, though, that he never once said that he's been wanting for money since he became famous ... because by the looks of it, he hasn't. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Roth is worth a very respectable $60 million. So, if he's had that kind of money sitting around all along, why did he do the whole EMT thing? "I live a very different life away from music," he says.
